What is "Project Lost in Time"?


Project Lost in Time (Or shortened to Project LiT or ProjLiT), is an Infogrames documentation fan site, made for preserving their Looney Tunes games.
Everything for the story, levels, OST, art, etc. will be preserved and documented here.



What games will be featured?

The games that will be featured are all 13 Looney Tunes games published by Infogrames, and those are:

  • Bugs Bunny: Lost in Time
  • Bugs Bunny and Taz: Time Busters
  • Sheep Dog n' Wolf (Sheep Raider)
  • Looney Tunes Racing
  • Taz: Wanted
  • Taz Express
  • Duck Dodgers Starring Daffy Duck
  • Looney Tunes: Space Race
  • Loons: The Fight For Fame
  • Looney Tunes: Marvin Strikes Back!
  • Looney Tunes Collector: Alert!
  • Bugs Bunny & Lola Bunny: Operation Carrot Patch
  • Looney Tunes: Twouble!
