What is "Project Lost in Time"?


Project Lost in Time (Or shortened to Project LiT or ProjLiT), is an Infogrames documentation fan site, made for preserving their Looney Tunes games.
Everything for the story, levels, OST, art, etc. will be preserved and documented here.



What games will be featured?

The games that will be featured are all 13 Looney Tunes games published by Infogrames, and those are:

  • Bugs Bunny: Lost in Time (1999)
  • Bugs Bunny and Taz: Time Busters (2000)
  • Sheep Dog n' Wolf (Sheep Raider) (2001)
  • Looney Tunes Racing (2000)
  • Taz: Wanted (2002)
  • Taz Express (2000)
  • Duck Dodgers Starring Daffy Duck (2000)
  • Looney Tunes: Space Race (2000, 2002)
  • Loons: The Fight For Fame (2002)
  • Looney Tunes: Marvin Strikes Back! (2000)
  • Looney Tunes Collector: Alert! (2000)
  • Bugs Bunny & Lola Bunny: Operation Carrot Patch (1998)
  • Looney Tunes: Twouble!(Sylvester & Tweety: Breakfast on the Run) (1998)


Will other games besides those one be featured?

Probably. There are many games on my mind that I want to document that are not Looney Tunes.
But honestly, only time will tell.
If other games will be covered here, it will probably be other, somewhat unknown mascot platformers (Something like Donald Duck: Quack Attack). But like I said, only time will tell.

Why are you making this project?

Just for the fun of it, honestly. I am a big Looney Tunes fan, and I am an even bigger fan of the games published by Infogrames.
I played them when I was little, and I still come back to them, they hold a special place in my heart.
So when I found out that basically no one remembers the game, and that's one of the biggest reasons as to why I'm making this project. To make the games at least little bit more popular.
And also this is my first ever web dev project, and I'm a beginner at this, so I figured why not make a site documenting the games that I love so much.
