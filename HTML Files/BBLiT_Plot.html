Bugs Bunny: Lost in Time

Plot




The game starts with Bugs Bunny going on vacation to Pismo Beach. But he accidentally takes a wrong turn at Alberquerque and ends up in an abandonded shed with a time travel machine.


After some time Bugs realises where he is, and he mistakes the time travel machine for a carrot juice dispenser. After accidentally activating it, he gets transported through time.


He ends up in Nowhere, the home of a sorcerer named Merlin Munroe. He informs Bugs that he is lost in time and he must travel through different eras of time to collect clock symbols and golden carrots that allow him to return to the present.
