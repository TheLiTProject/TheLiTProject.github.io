Bugs Bunny: Lost in Time

Introduction




Bunny: Lost in Time (Shortened to BB:LiT) is a Looney Tunes 3D platformer video game made for the Sony PlayStation in 1999. It was later ported to Windows systems the same year.


The game was made by Behaviour Interactive and it was published by Infogrames.


PlayStation

NA: May 31, 1999

EU: June 29, 1999

Windows

NA: September 30, 1999

EU: 1999 (Exact date unknown)
